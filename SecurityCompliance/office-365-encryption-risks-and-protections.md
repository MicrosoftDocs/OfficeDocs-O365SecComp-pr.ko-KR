Microsoft implements a large set of technology and process-based methods (referred to as controls) to mitigate these risks. Identification, evaluation and mitigation of risks via controls is a continuous process. The implementation of controls within various layers of our cloud services such as facilities, network, servers, applications, users (such as Microsoft administrators) and data form a defense-in-depth strategy. The key to this strategy is that many different controls are implemented at different layers to protect against the same or similar risk scenarios. Some risk scenarios and the currently available encryption technologies that mitigate them are listed below. These scenarios are in many cases also mitigated via other controls implemented in Office 365.</span></span>
